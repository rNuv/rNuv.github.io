Welcome To ParkWork!

Park Work is the next step towards implementing fitness into your everyday life.
With the usage of QR coding, you can scan various barcodes around parks, and perform the activity that is displayed.
You can perform these excercises by yourself, or with a buddy!
With the use of Apple Maps, you can check the location of the nearest fitpit, so you can plan out your exercising!
